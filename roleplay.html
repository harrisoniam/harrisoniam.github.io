This Is Homigrad

> The way it was meant to be played.

Homigrad was created with the intent for players to roleplay and react to the world realistically as it changed and evolved around them.
This server enforces it.

You are REQUIRED to
roleplay your Character.


Scream in agony from the wounds given to your character, weep the loss of a friend that you gained during the round, and act accordingly with what's happening around you and to the world.


If roleplay does not interest you, please play on one of our other servers, where roleplay is not enforced.
