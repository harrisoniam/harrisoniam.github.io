Welcome to Harrison's Z-City Server!

Please read the below rules and tips to ensure a smooth playing experience.

  1. Be kind. We're all here to have a good time.
  2. Any form of toxicity, hate speech, or harassment, direct or otherwise, will result in a ban.
  3. Mic Spam, such as loud noises, continous music, and excessive soundboard effects, is not allowed.
  4. Cheats and/or exploits are not allowed, and will result in a ban.
  5. RDM & Team Killing is not allowed.

Useful Binds & Tips

There are several binds in Z-City that will improve your experience. You can use the below commands in the Garry's Mod console to configure these binds. Replace [KEY] with your desired input.

  • bind [KEY] fake to ragdoll and unragdoll.
  • bind [KEY] suicide to attempt suicide. Must have a weapon to do so!
  • bind [KEY] hg_kick to perform a kick attack.
  • bind [KEY] "say *drop" to drop your currently equipped item.
